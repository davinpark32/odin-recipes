Carbonara

Description

Classic Italian Carbonara, taught by late Antonio Carluccio.

Ingredients

  • 220g Spaghetti
  • 25g Guanciale or pancetta
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 3 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesean cheese freshly grated
  • Freshly grouund black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the water, put 10g of salt per litre of water.
  2. Immediately put the pasta in the water.
  3. Cut pancetta in chunks.
  4. Put olive oil and pancetta in the pan.
  5. Take 2 eggs and 1 yolk and beat it.
  6. Add parmesan and pepper, then stir.
  7. Reduce the heat on the pancetta, remove some oil and switch it off.
  8. Put pasta into the pan and let it cool down.
  9. Add the sauce and stir it.
  10. Add parmesan and touch of pepper for the decoration.
